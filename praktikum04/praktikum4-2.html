HOW TO MAKE SWEET BREAD

SWEET BREAD

RECIPE

Cooking Tools

  • Bowl
  • Mixer
  • Mixing Bowl
  • Rolling Pin
  • Pan
  • Oven

Ingredients

  • Twin chakra flour 800gr
  • Blue triangle flour 200gr
  • Ice water 400gr
  • Yeast 15gr
  • Salt 15gr
  • Sugar 200gr
  • Butter/margarine 180gr
  • Milk powder 40gr
  • Bread improver 5gr
  • Egg yolk 1 butir
  • Whole eggs 2 butir
  • Honey 30gr
  • Emulsifier 10gr

Method

  • 1. Stir the dry ingredients until well blended
  • 2. Add egg yolks, eggs, honey and water. Stir until well mixed
  • 3. After mixed well, add margarine and emulsifier and stir until smooth
  • 4. Rest 5-10 minutes. And cover with plastic
  • 5. Cut and weigh about 7 grams, round it back
  • 6. Rest 10 minutes
  • 7. Fill in the toppings
  • 8. Proofung approximately 30 minutes
  • 9. Bake at a temperature of approximately 210 degrees Celsius for 5-8 minutes
